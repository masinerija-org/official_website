MAŠINERIJA (MACHINERY, МАШИНЕРИЈА) is an organisation of professionals in Data Science, Analytics, and Engineering, ML, AI, and MLOps, in Serbia.

## Our Mission

Our core mission is to sustain and nurture a vibrant professional community across the data science and AI landscape in Serbia. We bring together industry practitioners to:

- Share knowledge, expertise, and hands-on experience across disciplines
- Organise regular events, meetups, and conferences that connect professionals and foster collaboration
- Inform and engage the public on the social, ethical, and policy implications of AI and emerging data technologies
- Advance standards and best practices that strengthen the maturity of our industry

Together, we work to ensure that the data and AI community in Serbia remains connected, informed, and forward-looking.
