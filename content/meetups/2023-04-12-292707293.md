<p><strong>Generative AI in Action: Three case studies with OpenAI GPT(3-3.5) in Serbian, Slovenian, and Croatian</strong></p>
<p><strong>Goran S. Milovanović, Phd, Smartocto/DataKolektiv/МАШИНЕРИЈА</strong></p>
<p><strong>ABSTRACT.</strong> This talk will cover the application of OpenAI GPT models (mostly) in the Serbian language for text classification, sentiment analysis, and user intent recognition. The speaker will present three case studies and review the programmatic work with OpenAI GPT-3.5-turbo and GPT-3 (ada and curie) generative models. The talk will also cover the use of the Python openai library and the OpenAI CLI for working with these models. Fine-tuning of GPT-3 model(s) for text classification will be covered as well, alongside the principles of prompt engineering for conversational models (gpt-3.5-turbo). Attendees can expect to gain insights into the practical applications of OpenAI GPT models and how they can be used for language tasks in Serbian and other languages. Some astonishing results in the sentiment analysis of the text in Serbian, Croatian, and Slovenian language will be presented, on a sample of more than 6000 recent newspaper articles and blogs.</p>
