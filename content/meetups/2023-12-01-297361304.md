<p><strong>DATA SCIENCE CLUB #17</strong></p>
<p><strong>Reinforcement Learning in StarCraft 2</strong></p>
<p>StarCraft 2 is a complex game, and teaching an AI to master it can be a daunting task. Instead, let's focus on training it to play in limited, smaller scenarios that replicate specific aspects of the core game. Utilizing Deep Q-learning and the StarCraft 2 Python library to operate the game, we will explore in detail the requirements and processes involved in learning to play StarCraft 2.</p>
