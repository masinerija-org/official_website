<p><strong>Efficient language model inference</strong></p>
<p>Transformer-based language models are widely used for natural language processing tasks. Although they are powerful, more resources are required to train and use them. The extremely high inference cost, in both memory and time, is a bottleneck for usign such models for real-world tasks.</p>
<p>The talk will cover different methods for memory and time efficient inference, how to get the most out of model training and memory-time-accuracy tradeoff.</p>
