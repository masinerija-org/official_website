<p><strong>Abstract</strong></p>
<p>Discover the DVCLive a Python API module to streamline experiment tracking and collaboration with DVC. We'll guide you through easy metrics, plots, and artifacts tracking and collaboration using Jupyter Notebooks and VSCode IDE. Master experiment persistence and sharing. Supercharge your ML workflow with DVCLive!</p>
<p>Key takeaways:</p>
<ul>
<li>DVCLive is the easiest way to start with DVC</li>
<li>You may log metrics, plots, and artifacts from Jupiter Notebooks</li>
<li>You may visualize metrics and compare experiments inside VSCode IDE</li>
<li>No additional tools and tracking servers are needed!</li>
</ul>
