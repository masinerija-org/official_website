<p><strong>Title: Chemistry AI. Recommendation system for organic chemistry</strong></p>
<p><strong>Abstract:</strong> Here we consider one case of a chemistry task for AI. We touch on the understanding of synthesis, and what problems exist in this area. Also here is shown what data in organic chemistry can be found, how to bring them into a form that the model can understand, and what we want to get as output. Also, we consider existing solutions, their limitations and what problems we face.</p>
<p>This talk is more about getting a chance to expand your horizons in AI and to brainstorm possible architecture of NN.</p>
